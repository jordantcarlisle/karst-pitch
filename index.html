Risk Mitigation

We've thought about what could go wrong.

Operator underperformance
Long-term lease with personal guarantees. Building designed for multi-tenant wellness use. Core thermal/fitness infrastructure retains value.
Slow membership ramp
Break-even at 600-700 members (Year 1-2 achievable). Day passes and corporate events provide cash flow during ramp.
Construction overrun
Phased scope. Underground cave can be Phase 2. Each phase independently viable.
Market timing
Wellness gap exists now. Concept viable independent of Ice House timeline, but 2028 co-launch maximizes impact.
Underground complexity
Geotechnical assessment is Phase 1 due diligence. If unfavorable, thermal program redesigned as ground-level wing.
